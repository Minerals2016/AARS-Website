What is AARS?

Our project is called AARS; short for Automatic Attendance Recording System. It takes a collaborative effort of hardware and software to power a system which is complex yet efficient. It is aimed at teachers and school administrators who want to save time by automating attendance. It will reduce errors and make instruction faster and distraction free. Can I Recreate or Alter AARS?

Yes you can! AARS is a free and open source project. We charge on you implementing our hardware and using our own data storage but the software is always free.
